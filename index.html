Mission:
We study computational and crowdsourcing tools that will renovate engineering and industrial design activities. Specifically, we consider design problem as finding an optimal solution from a potentially high-dimensional space and investigate novel integrations of human knowledge, learning algorithms and physics-based models to enable more effective and efficient search of design solutions. Challenges lie across multiple disciplines including optimization, machine learning, crowdsourcing, and engineering system design and control. Key real-world applications include computational material design, powertrain design and control, over-actuated robot system design and control, and automated shape design for functionalities and preferences.
