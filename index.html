Mission:
We study computational and crowdsourcing tools that will renovate engineering and industrial design activities. Specifically, we consider any design problem as finding an optimal solution from a potentially high-dimensional space and investigate effective and efficient search algorithms that integrate human knowledge, learning algorithms and physics-based models. Challenges lie across optimization, machine learning, crowdsourcing, and engineering system design and control. Key real-world applications include powertrain design and control, computational material design, over-actuated robot system design and control, and automated shape design for functionalities and preferences.
