Bio

I am currently an associate professor with the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Arizona State University. I received my B.S. in Automotive Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2007 and my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, in 2012, under the guidance of Dr. Panos Papalambros.

Research Interests

I'm interested in open questions related to game theory, optimization, and AI for science that are technically within my reach. Current projects include:

  • 2p0s differential games with incomplete information and temporal logic specifications (e.g., for football strategic planning)
  • Learning PDEs from experimental observations (e.g., for modeling materials structure-property mapping)
  • GenAI robust watermarking and attribution
